The design shows an updated blog article template for Senior Healthcare Direct, a Medicare information website powered by AmeriLife.
The template is applied to the article "Medicare Advantage Open Enrollment Period."

The page opens with a site header carrying a licensed-agent phone number and call-to-action button, a primary navigation bar, and a breadcrumb trail.
Below the article title and summary sits a full-width hero photograph, followed by the author byline, topic tags, and social share icons.
The body copy runs alongside a sidebar callout promoting a related explainer video.
An email subscription banner for "Medicare News" sits beneath the article body, above a duplicated block of the article text.
