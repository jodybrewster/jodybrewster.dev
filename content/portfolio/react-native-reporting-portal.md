React Native Reporting Portal

This is a React Native tablet app built for Lennar, the largest US homebuilder, giving employees a single mobile home for the company's internal business reporting dashboards.
Reports are organized under a persistent row of category tabs, including Favorites, Recently Opened, All Reports, Regional President, Division President, Finance & Accounting, Human Resources, Operations & Construction, Customer Care, Sales & Marketing, and Land.
Each report appears as a list row with a short description, a star toggle for favoriting, and an overflow menu, and the list can be searched or sorted from the header.
Opening a report's overflow menu shows an info panel with its full description, a "Lennar Certified" badge, how many people have favorited it, its data refresh interval for each connected source system, and its functional area.
The app supports both light and dark appearance modes and opens with a branded splash screen showing the Lennar Reporting Portal logo on a blue-to-purple gradient.
