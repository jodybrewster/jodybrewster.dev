Senior Healthcare Direct is a Medicare information website powered by AmeriLife.
This project is a blog page update for the site.
The single design shown is a redesigned blog article template, applied to the article "Medicare Advantage Open Enrollment Period."

The layout includes the site header with a licensed-agent phone number and call-to-action, primary navigation, and breadcrumb trail.
Below the article title and summary sits a full-width hero photograph, followed by the author byline, topic tags, and social share icons.
The body copy runs alongside a sidebar video callout promoting a related explainer video.
An email subscription banner for "Medicare News" sits beneath the article body.
