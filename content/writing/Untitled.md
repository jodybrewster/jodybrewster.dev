I'm a principal experience architect.  I decided to start writing again on my site because I realized that its incredibly important to build context about yourself for your agents.  I can literally have agents build out this blog which I plan on doing. But first I want to create a post about who I am.  First off I started out as an artist.  I was really into graffiti when I was young and trying to up one another was a thing back then.  Who had better tags, writing on walls, etc.  School wasn't exactly about creating artists so I was very anti everything. 

One day my parents drove me up to Ringling  to see if I could get into the 3D artists program.  The counselor there said that I needed to be a valedictorian in math and that's what Pixar was looking for.  She asked if I wanted to go into the graphic design program but I really wanted to be doing more digital work.  Long story short, bought a computer with no job and still in high school. Went into debt, but started learning how to create art and publish it on the web.

That's how I started.  Landed a job in web design for a startup.  I had a lot of really smart people not only teach me Java but I worked on the first distributed database replication technology with a bunch of patents using this incredible new tech called XML. 

I've had a couple stints in startups.
